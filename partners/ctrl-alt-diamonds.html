üìãExecutive Summary

Ctrl Alt Diamonds represents a revolutionary advancement in alternative asset investment, bringing the multi-billion dollar diamond market onto the blockchain. Through a strategic partnership with Dubai-based Billiton Diamond and Ripple Custody, the platform enables fractional ownership of certified polished diamonds, democratizing access to one of the world's most prestigious tangible asset classes. This initiative marks the first time a major diamond dealer has partnered with blockchain infrastructure to tokenize certified gemstones.

The global diamond market represents approximately $87 billion annually, with Dubai serving as one of the world's premier diamond trading hubs. The city's strategic positioning, combined with its tax-free environment and world-class storage facilities, has made it a natural center for diamond commerce. Ctrl Alt's tokenization platform addresses long-standing barriers to diamond investment, enabling investors to acquire fractions of certified diamonds starting from approximately $1,000 USD.

The platform leverages Ripple Custody for institutional-grade storage and security, while the XRP Ledger provides proven blockchain infrastructure for token issuance, transfer, and trading. Each diamond token is backed by a physical stone with verified GIA (Gemological Institute of America) certification, ensuring transparency and authenticity. The integration of on-chain provenance tracking creates an unprecedented level of security and verifiability for diamond ownership.

Technical Architecture

‚öôÔ∏èToken Implementation on XRP Ledger

1

Diamond Token Issuance

Each unique diamond is represented by a non-fungible token (NFT) on XRPL, with detailed attributes including GIA certificate number, 4Cs specifications, and provenance records. Fractional shares are issued as fungible tokens backed by the NFT.

2

GIA Verification Hook

Token metadata includes GIA certificate hash, enabling automatic verification of diamond authenticity through the XRPL metadata extension. Certificate verification occurs at each transfer.

3

Custody Integration

Ripple Custody maintains the physical diamonds with regular audits. On-chain settlement triggers corresponding physical asset transfers, managed through institutional custody protocols.

4

Provenance On-Chain

Every ownership change is recorded with full audit trail. Historical provenance from mine to current holder is publicly verifiable on XRPL.
