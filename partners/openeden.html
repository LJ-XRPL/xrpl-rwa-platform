üìä TBILL Product Overview

OpenEden's TBILL product represents direct ownership of a diversified portfolio of short-term U.S. Treasury bills, the safest and most liquid sovereign debt in the world. The product is structured as a BVI mutual fund, with each token representing a proportional ownership interest in the underlying T-bill portfolio. Unlike fund-based structures that involve multiple layers of management fees and operational complexity, TBILL provides direct exposure to the yield curve with complete transparency into portfolio holdings.

The portfolio maintains a conservative investment approach, focusing on Treasury bills with maturities of 4, 13, 26, and 52 weeks. The average weighted maturity is approximately 30-45 days, providing stability while capturing the current high yields available in the short-term Treasury market. Interest accrues daily and is reflected in the token balance, ensuring that investors receive their proportional share of Treasury income without any delay or administrative friction.

  • Direct T-bill ownership
  • BVI mutual fund structure
  • Daily interest accrual
  • 30-45 day avg maturity
  • 100% U.S. Treasury backed

üè¶ Moody's-Rated Custody

A distinguishing feature of OpenEden's offering is the Moody's-rated custody solution, which provides institutional-grade protection for the underlying T-bill holdings. The custody arrangement ensures that all Treasury securities are held in segregated accounts with a top-tier custodian, with independent verification of holdings at regular intervals. This transparency addresses a critical concern in the tokenized assets space: the risk that underlying assets may not be properly safeguarded.

Moody's rating of the custody solution reflects the custodian's financial strength, operational capabilities, and regulatory compliance. The rating agency evaluates factors including capital adequacy, risk management practices, technological infrastructure, and historical performance. For investors, the Moody's rating provides independent verification that their assets are protected to the highest standards, reducing the counterparty risk that often concerns institutional allocators.

  • Moody's Aaa rating
  • Segregated accounts
  • Independent verification
  • Top-tier custodian
  • Full collateralization

‚è∞ 24/7 Mint/Redeem Mechanics

OpenEden has implemented sophisticated 24/7 minting and redemption mechanics that enable investors to enter or exit positions at any time, without the limitations of traditional fund structures. The minting process involves on-chain transaction execution, with new tokens created when investors send USD (or supported stablecoins) to the OpenEden treasury. The redemption process burns tokens and returns the corresponding value in underlying assets, typically within the same business day.

The technical implementation leverages smart contracts to manage the minting and redemption process, with oracle price feeds providing real-time NAV calculations. Authorized participants facilitate large-scale minting and redemption, while retail investors can access the product through secondary market purchases. This dual-channel approach ensures liquidity at various size levels, from small retail positions to large institutional allocations.

  • 24/7 on-chain minting
  • Same-day redemption
  • Smart contract automation
  • Real-time NAV oracles
  • Multi-channel access

üáªüá¨ BVI Regulatory Framework

OpenEden operates under the regulatory framework of the British Virgin Islands, a well-established offshore financial center with a sophisticated approach to digital asset regulation. The BVI Financial Services Commission (FSC) oversees mutual funds and investment business, providing regulatory oversight that balances investor protection with operational flexibility. The BVI's regulatory framework is recognized by international standards and maintains bilateral agreements with major jurisdictions.

The BVI mutual fund structure provides several advantages: flexible capital requirements, efficient registration process, and access to a well-developed legal system based on English common law. For OpenEden, this structure enables rapid product development and market entry while maintaining compliance with international regulatory standards. The BVI framework also provides tax efficiency, with no corporate income tax, capital gains tax, or withholding tax on distributions.

  • FSC regulatory oversight
  • Mutual fund license
  • English common law
  • Tax efficient structure
  • International recognition
