Technical Architecture: XRPL Integration

MarketNode's technical architecture leverages XRPL's unique capabilities to deliver institutional-grade tokenization with regulatory compliance, security, and operational efficiency at its core. The architecture follows a "token-and-custody" model that bridges traditional finance with blockchain innovation.

1

Fund Setup & Token Creation

Maybank establishes the MMF with regulatory approval from Bank Negara Malaysia and Securities Commission Malaysia. MarketNode creates the tokenized representation on XRPL using the Issued Currency mechanism. Each token is denominated with high precision (up to 16 decimal places) enabling fractional ownership down to minute fractions of a cent.

2

Custody & Asset Servicing (BNP Paribas)

BNP Paribas Securities Services acts as primary custodian, holding the underlying MMF assets off-chain in segregated accounts. They maintain the official share register and provide daily NAV calculations. This "token-and-custody" model ensures regulatory compliance while enabling blockchain benefits.

3

Investor Onboarding & KYC

Institutional investors complete regulated KYC/AML procedures through their existing banking relationships. MarketNode implements permissioned minting‚Äîonly verified institutional wallets can receive newly issued tokens. This prevents retail speculation while maintaining accessibility for legitimate institutional participants.

4

Primary Issuance & Redemption

Investors purchase tokens directly from the issuer (Maybank) via traditional banking channels, with tokens minted on XRPL upon settlement. Redemptions work in reverse‚Äîtokens are burned, and funds are settled through traditional banking. This hybrid model ensures regulatory certainty while gaining blockchain efficiency.

5

Secondary Trading on XRPL

Once acquired, tokens can be transferred between permissioned institutional wallets on the XRPL DEX. The distributed ledger provides instant settlement (4-second finality), complete transaction history, and programmatic compliance checks. Trading is restricted to the permissioned investor pool.

6

NAV Reporting & Transparency

MarketNode publishes daily NAV updates on-chain via IPFS or oracle services. Investors can verify their theoretical portfolio value at any time. BNP Paribas provides monthly statements and annual audit reports, bridging traditional finance accountability with blockchain transparency.

Risk Factors & Mitigation Strategies

Counterparty Risk
Risk: Default or insolvency of the MMF issuer (Maybank) or custodian (BNP Paribas) could impact token holders.

Mitigation: Both institutions are among the world's largest and most regulated. Maybank is rated A- by S&P; BNP Paribas has $13T+ AUC. Assets are segregated per regulatory requirements. Both institutions have existed for over 100 years.
Regulatory Risk
Risk: Changes in Malaysian, Singaporean, or APAC regulations could impact token issuance, distribution, or trading.

Mitigation: The consortium includes regulatory expertise and maintains active dialogue with Bank Negara Malaysia, Securities Commission, and MAS. Product structured to comply with existing frameworks.
Liquidity Risk
Risk: Tokenized MMF may have less liquidity than traditional MMF shares, especially during market stress.

Mitigation: Permissioned investor pool includes major banks. XRPL DEX enables secondary trading. Issuer maintains redemption facility per fund terms providing floor liquidity.
Operational Risk
Risk: Technology failures, smart contract bugs, or blockchain network issues could disrupt operations.

Mitigation: XRPL has 12+ year operational track record. Smart contracts undergo formal verification. Disaster recovery procedures in place at all partner institutions.
Market & Yield Risk
Risk: MMF yields may decline due to central bank policy changes or market conditions affecting short-term interest rates.

Mitigation: MMF is a short-term instrument with low duration risk. Yields follow market rates. Investors have 24/7 visibility into current yields via on-chain data.
Custody & Safekeeping Risk
Risk: Physical or digital asset loss due to custodian negligence or security breach.

Mitigation: BNP Paribas is a Tier-1 global custodian with robust controls, insurance coverage, and regulatory oversight. Assets segregated per regulations.
