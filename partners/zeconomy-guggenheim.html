Zeconomy and Guggenheim Treasury Services have partnered to create the first institutional-grade commercial paper product on the XRP Ledger. Commercial paper (CP) is a short-term unsecured debt instrument issued by corporations and financial institutions to meet short-term financing needs. The product offers investors access to high-quality corporate paper through a regulated structure, with yields competitive with money market funds but with enhanced transparency and 24/7 settlement capabilities.

The US commercial paper market is the world's largest and most liquid short-term debt market. Commercial paper is typically issued by large corporations with strong credit ratings, financial institutions, and sovereign entities to meet short-term working capital needs.

The market is characterized by high credit quality, with most outstanding paper rated A1/P1 (the highest short-term ratings). This makes commercial paper an attractive yield alternative to money market funds, with slightly higher returns but maintaining very low credit risk.

Yield Mechanics

The DCP tokens represent ownership interests in a portfolio of commercial paper instruments. Yields are generated from the interest payments on the underlying commercial paper, net of management fees.

Accrual Basis

Interest accrues daily based on the portfolio yield and is distributed periodically to token holders

Variable Rate

Yields float with market rates, providing current market returns as commercial paper rolls

Net of Fees

Stated yields are net of management fees, providing clear expected returns

Tax Efficiency

Interest income treatment provides potential tax advantages compared to capital gains
