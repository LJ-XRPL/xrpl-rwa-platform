Renewable Energy Contract Structure

  • Power Purchase Agreements (PPAs): Long-term contracts between energy generators and offtakers (utilities, corporations) guaranteeing fixed prices for renewable energy output
  • Contracted Cash Flows: PPAs provide predictable revenue streams backed by creditworthy offtakers, typically with 10-25 year terms
  • Project Finance Structure: Renewable energy projects are typically financed through a mix of debt and equity, with PPA contracts providing the cash flow security needed to service debt
  • Government Support: Argentina's renewable energy programs include tax incentives, subsidized financing, and priority grid access

Tokenization Mechanics

Each renewable energy project is tokenized as a separate tranche, with tokens representing proportional rights to the PPA cash flows. Investors receive predictable income distributions as the underlying projects generate and sell electricity, with distributions tied to actual energy production and contract terms.

Risk Factors

Regulatory Risk High

Argentina's regulatory environment can change rapidly. Changes to energy pricing, subsidy programs, or foreign investment rules could impact project economics and token valuations.

Currency Risk Medium

Argentine peso volatility may impact returns for USD-denominated investors, even when contract revenues are peso-denominated. Currency hedging strategies may be employed but carry costs.

Offtaker Credit Risk Medium

PPA payments depend on offtaker creditworthiness. WhileYPF is investment-grade, other offtakers may have lower credit ratings, creating counterparty risk.

Resource Risk Low

Wind and solar projects depend on natural resources. While long-term data is used in project finance, year-to-year variability in wind/solar resources can impact generation.

Liquidity Risk Medium

Secondary market for energy contract tokens may be less liquid than other asset classes. Large positions may face slippage when exiting.

Technology Risk Low

Wind and solar technologies are mature with proven track records. Operations and maintenance risks are well-understood and insured.
