1Executive Summary

Archax represents the gold standard for regulated digital securities exchanges in Europe, operating as the first FCA-authorized Multilateral Trading Facility (MTF) for digital assets in the United Kingdom. Founded in 2019 by Graham Rodford (formerly of Bloomberg and Liquid Markets), Archax has established itself as the bridge between traditional capital markets and blockchain-based assets, offering institutional investors a regulated pathway to access tokenized securities. The exchange's flagship product in the XRPL partnership is the tokenized abrdn USD Liquidity Fund, providing investors with regulated exposure to a premier money market fund with over $150 billion in assets under management.

The strategic significance of the Archax partnership for Ripple extends beyond product distribution‚Äîit represents regulatory validation of the XRP Ledger as a viable platform for institutional-grade securities. The FCA authorization provides a powerful endorsement that distinguishes XRPL from other blockchain platforms still navigating regulatory uncertainty. For institutional investors, Archax offers the rare combination of blockchain efficiency and traditional regulatory protections, including investor compensation scheme coverage and mandatory regulatory reporting.

From an investment perspective, the tokenized abrdn USD Liquidity Fund offers compelling advantages over both traditional money market funds and competing tokenized treasury products. The abrdn fund maintains a AAAm rating from Standard & Poor's, invests in high-quality short-term USD-denominated instruments including Treasury bills, commercial paper, and bank certificates of deposit, and has demonstrated consistent performance through multiple rate cycles. The tokenization layer adds 24/7 trading capability and fractional ownership, addressing key limitations of traditional fund structures while maintaining the regulatory protections that institutions require.

The UK regulatory framework provides Archax with significant competitive advantages in the European market. Following Brexit, the UK has carved out its own regulatory path for digital assets, with the FCA establishing clear guidelines for cryptoasset businesses. This regulatory clarity has attracted significant institutional interest, with UK-based asset managers and family offices increasingly allocating to tokenized securities. The Archax MTF operates under strict FCA rules regarding market abuse prevention, client asset protection, and operational resilience.

üèõÔ∏è FCA Regulatory Moat

Archax operates as an FCA-authorized Multilateral Trading Facility (MTF), providing a significant regulatory moat that distinguishes it from unregulated crypto exchanges and DeFi protocols. The MTF authorization requires compliance with extensive UK financial services regulations, including the Financial Services and Markets Act 2000 (FSMA), the FCA Handbook, and EU MiFID II requirements retained in UK law.

The FCA authorization provides several concrete protections for investors: mandatory client asset segregation (CASS rules), participation in the Financial Services Compensation Scheme (FSCS) for eligible claims up to ¬£85,000, regular regulatory reporting and audits, and compliance with market abuse regulations including MAR.

  • FCA MTF authorization
  • FSCS protection (up to ¬£85k)
  • CASS client asset rules
  • MiFID II compliance
  • Market abuse prevention (MAR)

üìà abrdn USD Liquidity Fund

The abrdn USD Liquidity Fund is a premier money market fund managed by abrdn plc, one of the world's largest investment companies. The fund invests in high-quality, short-term USD-denominated instruments including US Treasury bills, agency securities, commercial paper from prime issuers, and bank certificates of deposit. The fund maintains a AAAm rating from Standard & Poor's.

The fund's investment objective is to provide investors with current income consistent with preserving capital and maintaining liquidity. The portfolio maintains a weighted average maturity of 60 days or less and invests only in securities rated A-1/P-1 or higher.

üè¶ MTF Exchange Model

Archax operates as a Multilateral Trading Facility (MTF), a regulated exchange venue under MiFID II that brings together multiple third-party buying and selling interests. The MTF model provides significant advantages: transparent price discovery, real-time trade reporting to the FCA, automated trade execution, and centralized clearing through LCH.Clearnet.

For investors in the tokenized abrdn fund, this means reliable execution at published prices, reduced counterparty risk through central clearing, and the ability to view full order book depth before trading.

  • MTF under MiFID II
  • Central clearing (LCH)
  • Real-time trade reporting
  • Order book transparency
  • RFQ for large orders

üìú Digital Securities Framework

The UK has developed a comprehensive digital securities framework through the Financial Conduct Authority and the Bank of England's regulatory sandbox programs. This framework addresses key legal questions around the tokenization of securities, including the legal recognition of digital tokens as property and the treatment under existing company law.

Archax has worked closely with regulators to ensure its platform operates within this framework, implementing KYC, AML, and CTF measures that satisfy FCA requirements.

MTF vs DEX: Key Differences

Unlike decentralized exchanges (DEX), Archax's MTF provides centralized price discovery, central clearing through LCH, regulatory protection under FCA rules, and investor compensation through FSCS. This hybrid approach delivers blockchain efficiency with traditional regulatory protections.
